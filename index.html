Bio

A computer engineer, Marco Martinelli is a PhD Student at the Department of Information Engineering of the University of Padua. He took the Bachelor Degree in Computer Science Engineering and the Master Degree in Computer Engineering. His research spans Biomedical Natural Language Processing (BioNLP), Knowledge Base Construction (KBC), and Federated Learning.

Latest appointments:

  • Visiting student researcher at the Applied Digital Transformation Laboratory (Adit-Lab) of the Polytechnic Institute of Viana do Castelo, from February to July 2023.
  • Winner of the "Mille e Una Lode" Scholarship established by the University of Padua to be awarded to the best students (about 3 percent for each course of study, based on the merit coefficient), received on December 5, 2023.
